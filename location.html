location

Shamshabad is in the Indian state of Telangana.

It is located in Shamshabad mandal of Rajendranagar revenue division.

The international airport of Hyderabad is located here.It is located within the 8 KM from periphery of GHMC.

Geography

Shamshabad is located at 17.2603°N 78.3969°E. It has an average elevation of 574 metres (1,886 ft)
